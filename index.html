Sidney Sullivan

Hi, I'm Sidney!

And I'm an award-winning strategic communicator with over 9 years of professional experience in the media and PA / PR industry — spanning both public and private sector environments.


In 2016, I attained a B.A. in Journalism after graduating from the University of Washington. Later that same year, I joined my spouse (an active-duty serviceman) in Alaska, and became a digital producer at NBC's KTUU-TV. While maintaining the station's website and social media channels, I turned daily stories using interactive data visualizations and graphics. Additionally, I served as an on-air talent for weekly data-related news segments.

My professional history also includes work within the public sector. From 2020 to 2022, I served as a public affairs specialist for U.S. Army Garrison Bavaria. During this time, I gained robust experience formulating and disseminating command information, crisis communication, community outreach, foreign media operation and strategic communication plans and products for the largest military training area in Europe.

Following another military-related move to Asia, I have been serving as the community relations officer for Marine Corps Installations Pacific-MCB Camp Butler since 2023.

By April 2025, I will have obtained a M.S. in Strategic Communication from the University of Maryland Global Campus. From my graduate program, relavent sample works are highlighted below:
