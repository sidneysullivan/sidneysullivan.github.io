Sidney Sullivan

Hello! My name is Sidney.

And I'm a data journalist living in Bavaria, Germany.


As such, my expertise spans online reporting, computer programming, analyzing large data sets and creating interactive graphics. I also take an interest in analyzing social media and user engagement.

In 2016, I attained a B.A. in Journalism after graduating from the University of Washington. And later that same year, I found work as a digital producer at NBC's KTUU-TV, in Alaska. While maintaining the station's website, I turned daily stories using interactive data visualizations and graphics. Additionally, I served as an on-air talent for weekly data-related news segments.

My professional experience also includes work as: a freelance data analyst for NPR's KUOW-FM, a freelance journalist for several non-profit news organizations and an intern for Dataminir, Inc. — all located in Seattle, Washington.

Following a recent move to Eastern Germany, I am currently available for freelance work.

If you want to collaborate on a project, or just say hello, then let's connect!
