Sidney Sullivan

Hello! My name is Sidney.

And I'm a freelance data journalist living in Bavaria, Germany.


As such, my expertise spans online reporting, computer programming, analyzing large data sets and creating interactive graphics. I also take an interest in analyzing social media and user engagement.

I recently moved to Eastern Germany from the Pacific Northwest, USA. But before the move: I grew up in California, I earned my B.A. in Journalism from the University of Washington, and I found work as a digital producer at Channel 2 News KTUU-TV in Alaska.

My professional experience also includes freelance data work with KUOW-FM Public Radio, freelance journalism work with non-profit news organizations and an internship with Dataminir, Inc. - all located in Seattle.

If you would like to collaborate on a project, or just say hello, then let's connect!
