Sidney Sullivan

Hi, I'm Sidney!

And I'm an award winning strategic communicator with over 9 years of professional experience in the media and PA / PR industry — spanning both public and private sector environments.


In 2016, I attained a B.A. in Journalism after graduating from the University of Washington. Later that same year, I joined my spouse (an active-duty serviceman) in Alaska, and became a digital producer at NBC-affiliate station Channel 2 News KTUU-TV. While maintaining the station's website and social media channels, I produced award-winning data journalism and interactive data visualizations. To help viewers further understand the datasets and findings, I also served as an on-air talent for weekly data-related news segments.

While I am comfortable working in high-stress corporate environments to meet short deadlines, my professional history also includes work within the public sector. From 2020 to 2022, I served as a public affairs specialist for U.S. Army Garrison Bavaria. During this time, I gained robust experience formulating and disseminating command information, crisis communication, community outreach, foreign media operation and strategic communication plans and products for the largest military training area in Europe. And following another military-related move to Asia, I currently serve as the community relations officer for Marine Corps Installations Pacific-MCB Camp Butler.
